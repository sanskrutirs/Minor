Rmageddon is a intra college robotics event organised by our team.

For more information of our club click on below link.

    Gests and Honor

    Vijay Kumar Chief Guest

    -Robotics Engineer

    Kumar is known for his research in the control and coordination of multi-robot formations.He served as the assistant director of robotics and cyber physical systems at the White House Office of Science and Technology Policy.

    Dr. Y. V. Joshi Honor

    -Director of SGGSIE&T

    Prof. Y. V. Joshi completed Bachelor of Electronics Engineering in 1986, Masters of Electronics Engineering in 1991 and a Ph.D. in 1998. He is member of IEEE, Fellow of Institution of Engineers, Fellow of IETE and a life member of ISTE.

    Dr. Suhas Gajre Honor
